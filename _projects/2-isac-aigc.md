This project studies resource allocation for ISAC-driven AIGC services, where sensing accuracy, generation quality, computing resources, and communication resources jointly affect user experience.

## Research problem

How can an ISAC-enabled network allocate sensing, computing, and communication resources while accounting for both content accuracy and content quality in AIGC services?

## My role

Lead researcher and first author.

## Methods

- Integrated sensing and communication
- AIGC service quality modeling
- Sensing-computing-communication resource allocation
- LP-guided deep reinforcement learning

## Main results

The proposed method improved the content-accuracy-and-quality-aware metric by more than 10% over learning baselines and by more than 50% over schemes considering generation quality alone.
