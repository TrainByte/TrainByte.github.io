This project addresses cloud-edge scheduling for dynamically arriving AIGC tasks under limited edge resources.

- Build a cooperative cloud-edge model covering task offloading, computing allocation, and service latency.
- Develop a distributed hybrid quantum-classical reinforcement-learning framework.
- Use parameterized quantum circuits and classical neural networks for compact policy representation.
- Reduce policy-network parameters by more than 95% and average service delay by more than 30% in simulation.
