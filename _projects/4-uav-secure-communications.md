This project studies secure UAV-assisted mmWave MIMO communications under strong interference and anti-tracking scenarios.

## Research problem

How can a UAV-assisted mmWave MIMO system jointly optimize deceptive signaling, UAV position, beamforming, and power allocation to improve secure transmission?

## My role

Task leader, technical lead, and first author.

## Methods

- UAV positioning
- mmWave MIMO beamforming
- Deceptive signal design
- Power allocation
- Secure transmission optimization

## Main results

The proposed scheme achieved a performance gap of no more than 3% from the global optimum, improved secure transmission rate by more than 9% over direct-link transmission, and by more than 100% over the no-deception scheme.
