As task lead and technical lead, I studied UAV-assisted secure communications in strong-interference and anti-tracking scenarios.

- Model legitimate links, deceptive links, reactive jammers, and UAV-assisted relaying.
- Jointly optimize beamforming, three-dimensional UAV placement, and power allocation.
- Improve secure transmission rate to approximately twice that of the no-deception baseline.
- Produce a journal article and a granted invention patent.
